# Cashify – Own Again

**Cashify** is a conversational commerce tool powering **Own Again**, a business that simplifies item reselling and social media marketing.  
With Cashify, users can text product details and pictures just like in a normal conversation, and the system automatically posts them across **Own Again’s** digital channels — including **Instagram Stories & Reels, TikTok Feed, Facebook, and the Own Again website**.  

---

## ✨ Features

- **Conversational Item Posting**  
  Share product details and images via chat, no complex forms required.  

- **Automated Social Marketing**  
  Instantly publish posts to **Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and the Own Again website** for maximum visibility.  

- **Blockchain-Powered Security**  
  Buyers can **reserve/book items securely** using smart contracts.  
  - Funds are locked in a contract until the item is confirmed as received.  
  - Once confirmed, payment is automatically released to the seller’s wallet.  
  - Protects both buyer and seller with **trustless transactions**.  

- **Seamless Seller Experience**  
  No need to manually upload across multiple platforms — Cashify does the heavy lifting.
